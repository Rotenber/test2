2. Your informationYour information

Please note:

  • All visitors to the museum are required to show a valid entrance ticket.
  • It is not possible to store luggage in our cloakroom.
  • Purchased tickets are non-refundable.
  • Card holders will be asked to show their valid card.
  • The unique multimedia guide completes the museum experience for adults and visitors aged 13 and above.
  • Visitors with a youth ticket are required to show prove of age at the entrance of the museum.

* How your donation will be used:

  • To ensure that the legacy of Vincent van Gogh is – and remains – accessible to all, from disabled and visually-impaired visitors to children and the elderly.
  • To enable scientific research and the restoration of paintings.
